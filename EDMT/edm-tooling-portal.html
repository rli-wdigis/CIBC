Data Quality
Enterprise Data Management
Description

Data Quality Management is defined as the planning, implementation, and control of activities that apply quality management techniques to data, in order to assure it is fit for consumption and meet the intended use of a given business process or operation.

Effective Data Quality Management includes implementation of controls at the point of data collection or ingestion and measurement, reporting & remediation of data quality rules to demonstrate trust in the data for data consumers.

Data Roles
Enterprise Data Management
Description

It is essential to maintain these roles in most accurate & current state as they serve as the pillars to each & every process laid out for an effective data governance within CIBC.

Data Sharing
Enterprise Data Management
Description

Data Sharing is the process of distributing or providing access to data between individuals, or systems. Governance of the data sharing process ensures that data is transferred securely, and in compliance with CIBC’s Data Governance Policies.

A Data Sharing Agreement (DSA) is a formal document that outlines the terms and conditions under which data is shared between a Data Provider and a Data Consumer within CIBC.

Data Sharing Tool will streamline the approval process and enable Spokes to centrally host, track and manage their Data Sharing Agreements. This will improve tracking of data usage & ensure auditability.

Issue Management
Enterprise Data Management
Description

Issue Management is the systematic process of identifying, tracking, resolving, and monitoring issues that arise during a project or within an organization. It involves documenting problems, assigning responsibilities, prioritizing solutions, and ensuring timely resolution to minimize impacts.

The Enterprise Data Issue Management (EDIM) Tool is a robust cross-functional solution for effectively managing and overseeing Data Issues impacting data quality, integrity and management of data.
